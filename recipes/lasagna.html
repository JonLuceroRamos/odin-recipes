Description

This could possibly be the worst lasagna to ever lay upon your taste buds

I never said i was a chef so please don't judge this recipe too harshly

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

      • Steps

      1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
      2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
      3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat; drain excess fat.
      4. Add marinara sauce to the skillet with the beef; simmer for 5 minutes.
      5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper until well combined.
      6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
      7. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce, then spread half of the ricotta mixture on top.
      8. Add a layer of mozzarella cheese, then another layer of meat sauce.
      9. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with meat sauce and mozzarella on top.
      10. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
      11. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes; remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
      12. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
